Southern Signers

Thomas Jefferson
Principal author of the Declaration of Independence and later the third U.S. president.
John Adams
A strong advocate for independence, later the second U.S. president.
Benjamin Franklin
A key diplomat, scientist, and elder statesman involved in the independence movement.
John Hancock
President of the Continental Congress, famous for his large and bold signature.
Samuel Adams
Revolutionary leader and cousin of John Adams, known for organizing resistance against British policies.
Roger Sherman
Helped draft the Declaration of Independence and was part of the committee that wrote it.
Robert R. Livingston
Worked on the committee to draft the Declaration but was absent during the signing.
